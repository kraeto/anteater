Want to learn more about Quality Assurance? Keep reading!

The burning question is: What exactly is Quality Assurance?

It is the maintenance of a desired level of quality in a service or product, especially by means of attention to every stage of the process of delivery or production.

Quality assurance isn't just important for software testing. All companies should be able to test out their product or service before submitting it to consumers. Quality assurance looks different for each company and industry:

  • A chef for a family-owned restaurant tests out her new recipe with the restaurant owners in order to receive feedback
  • An event planner compares the layout of an upcoming party with the actual venue space
  • App developers hand out a beta version of their product to friends and family in order to perform a black-box test of the software
